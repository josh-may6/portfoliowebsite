About Me

Hey, I'm Josh! I've been happily married to my amazing wife, Makenzie, for five years. We share this awesome Golden-Doodle named Nellie who's just a ball of energy, keeping us on our toes and bringing so much joy into our lives. One of our standout memories together was our trip to Rome. The history, the culture, and the food were just incredible. Speaking of food, Makenzie and I are big fans of local breweries and Mexican cuisine. But our absolute favorite spot has to be Chi - Chinese and Sushi Bar. Bend, OR is where we call home. I love Bend for all the seasonal hobbies like snowboarding, rock climbing, running, and dirt biking. Lately, golf has become a new passion for me, and it's such a great way to enjoy the beauty of Bend. Weekends often involve some home DIY projects – there's always something to tinker with. Come Friday night, you'll likely find me at a local brewery with friends, soaking in the laid-back atmosphere and enjoying some quality time. And when we head back home, it's all about games and maybe mixing up a mean whiskey sour – the perfect way to cap off the week. Thank you for taking the time to get to know a bit about me. I’m eager for the chance to discuss how I can contribute my skills and positive energy as a valuable asset to your team.
